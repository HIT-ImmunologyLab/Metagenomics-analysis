Introduction

The Three Stages of CRISPR Immunity

Many bacterial and archaeal genomes have been sequenced, and a large fraction of them have CRISPR systems, ranging from deadly human pathogens to archaea living in the harshest environments on earth. Some CRISPR systems have been studied very well, and more is being discovered about CRISPR every day. CRISPRkb (CRISPR knowledge base) is a tool to collect this growing pool of information and generate versatile and useful annotations. These are some of the annotations we include:

(1)CRISPR system annotation

(2)CRISPR system classification

(3)Spacer self-matches to host genome

(4)inferring phage and bacteria infection networks

(5)Phage genome content near spacer matches
