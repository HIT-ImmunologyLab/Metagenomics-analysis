    Introduction

    •         CRISPRminer: A comprehensive and user-friendly knowledge base to explore CRISPR-systems in bacteria-phage interactions. This database seeks to gather and investigate the growing pool of important CRISPR information in this fast-growing field and transform it into meaningful annotations. Here are some annotations we include:

      1.Prediction (Discovery of CRISPR knowledge in the bacterial genomes)

      2.CRISPR-Cas (Look up pre-calculated CRISPR-Cas systems)

      3.Classification (Types and subtypes of the predicted CRISPR-Cas system)

      4.Self-targeting (Annotation of self-targeting spacers)

      5.Interaction (Bacteria and phage infection network)

      6.Anti-crisprs (Annotation of anti-crispr proteins and their homologs)
